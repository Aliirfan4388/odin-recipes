Perfect grilled beef steak recipe

A steak is a flavorful and tender cut of beef that's often enjoyed as a succulent main course. It's known for its juicy texture and rich taste, making it a favorite dish for meat lovers. Here's a concise list of ingredients and a brief recipe for cooking a steak:

ingredients

  • steak
  • salt
  • olive oil
  • butter

recipe

  1. Remove the steak from the refrigerator and let it sit at room temperature for 30 minutes.
  2. Pat the steak dry with paper towels to remove excess moisture.
  3. Season the steak generously with salt and black pepper on both sides. Add optional seasonings if desired.
  4. Heat a skillet or grill pan over high heat and add olive oil or butter.
  5. Sear the steak for 2-3 minutes per side for medium-rare (adjust cooking time for preferred doneness).
  6. Remove the steak from the pan and let it rest for 5 minutes on a cutting board.
  7. Slice the steak against the grain and serve with your favorite side dishes.
  8. This simple recipe ensures a deliciously cooked steak, showcasing the meat's natural flavors and tenderness. Enjoy!
