Lemonade

This straightforward recipe results in a classic, thirst-quenching lemonade, perfect for hot summer days or whenever you crave a citrusy delight. Enjoy!

Lemonade is a refreshing and tangy citrus beverage enjoyed worldwide. Here's a concise list of ingredients and a brief recipe for making lemonade:

ingredients

  • fresh water
  • sugar
  • lemon
  • ice cubes

recipe

  1. Squeeze fresh lemons to extract their juice. You'll need approximately 4-6 lemons to make 1 cup of lemon juice.
  2. In a pitcher, combine the freshly squeezed lemon juice with 1 cup of sugar.
  3. Boil 1 cup of water and pour it into the pitcher. Stir well until the sugar completely dissolves.
  4. Add 3-4 cups of cold water to the pitcher, depending on your desired level of sweetness and strength. Adjust to taste.
  5. If you prefer your lemonade cold, you can either chill it in the refrigerator or add ice cubes to the pitcher.
  6. Stir the lemonade again to ensure the ingredients are well-mixed.
  7. Serve your homemade lemonade in glasses filled with ice cubes, garnish with lemon slices if desired.
